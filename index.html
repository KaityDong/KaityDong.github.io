Kaity Dong

Political Science/International Affairs & Labor Studies

Kaity Dong is a fourth year at UC Riverside studying Political Science/International Affairs with a minor in Labor Studies. She is interested in community advocacy, policy, and sustainability. She has worked on a number of projects and missions, having been a part of nonprofits such as APIAVote and Young Invincibles. She's contributed her passions to spaces in both Sacramento, CA and Washington, D.C..

As a graduating college student, Kaity Dong is interested in pursing work aligning with her interests in community service. Professionally, she is currently a Young Invincible's 'YAP' Fellow, APIAVote Ambassador, and Young People's Alliance Chapter Lead and Founder. In her own time, she volunteers at Riverside Youth Judo Club as a Youth mentor and at Gable Farms as a CollegeCorps Fellow.
